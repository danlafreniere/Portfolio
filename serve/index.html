ABOUT ME

I am a recent graduate of the second-degree Computing program at Queen's University. I'm currently preparing for job interviews after taking some time off and will be available for hire beginning January 2017. I have recently completed a summer position at Queen's IT services where I worked with clients to build and maintain websites for their respective faculties. I built up strong rapport with my clients over the summer and had the priviledge of freelancing with these awesome individuals at their request during my studies.

My first degree in psychology was obtained from the University of Alberta. I spent a number of years in the field of clinical neuropsychology where I studied human cognition and abnormal brain structure in patients suffering from schizophrenia via patient testing and MRI acquisition. Though I loved the field, I found myself spending a lot of my free time coding and decided that I should follow my heart and pursue computer science. My passions lie in artificial intelligence, software development and app/web development but I love learning new skills and facing the rewarding challenges that computer science presents to me every day.

I'm a huge music fan and dig everything from death metal to dream pop. When I have time I love travelling wherever the wind takes me or working on many of my growing side projects. I also enjoy kayaking, bouldering, snowboarding, and hiking in the mountains on my days off. My personal goals involve learning night photography, how to fly a helicopter, and building a smart home with A.I. integration.

I'm told I'm a very nice fella so don't hesitate to drop me a line!
