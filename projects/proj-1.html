Hypertension contributes to a variety of severe health conditions such as heart disease, stroke, and renal failure. Combined with a high prevalence, the disease creates enormous amounts of strain for healthcare systems. Using data associated with hypertension's contributing factors, I utilized machine learning techniques to predict presence of the disease in a very large sample of patients.

This research is currently being published in the 2016 IEEE Symposium Series in Computational Intelligence conference. An official manuscript is expected shortly.
